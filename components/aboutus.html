About Us

The Belgian Waffle Co. began in 2015 when Shrey,
inspired by international food trends,
brought the unique,delectable waffle concept to India.
our mission is built on excellence, simplicity, and affordability.

With over 450 stores in 160+ cities,
we've received accolades like the
"Franchisor of the Year" in the Dessert Category
and the "Asia Food Innovation Awards."
Our product line now includes Waffle Crisps, Spreads,
and Pancake and Waffle Premixes
